NEN Technologies OPC

Company Profile

NEN Technologies OPC is a corporation registered under Securities and Exchange Commission of the Philippines. We are also registered with Talisay City Hall and Bureau of Internal Revenue. Our story began in March 2011. Back then we were installing cable TV like Cignal and GSAT. On February 2018, we added new service which is installing CCTV or Surveilance cameras. We cater residential and commercial establishments. We registered our business on March 2018. NEN was derived from Hunter X Hunter movie which means " Constant Improvement". For this year 2021, we are planning to expand our business by selling electronics product and aircon. We are also planning to do aircon installation for both window type and split type.

Mission

Nen is aimed to provide a one stop shop electronics product and services to the people.

Vision

To become the Cebu's go to company in CCTV, Satellite TV and Gree Air Conditioning installation.
